middle mouse button - move

G - Move
R- Rotate
S - Scale

H - Hide
alt+H - UnHide
E - Extruding (kind of forms a line structure around blueprint)
ctrl+r - to add more points in plane polygon
shift + vertexs - to select multiple vertexes and then can merge
ctrl + vertexes + s - connects middle lines of mesh.
alt+s - to move mesh up down

1 - -Y Viewpoart
CTRL+1 - Y Viewpoart
3 - X Viewpoart
ctrl+3 - -X Viewpoart

7 - Z Viewpoart
ctrl+7 - -Z Viewpoart

TAB - Edit Mode On/Off
SHIFT + A - Shapes Menu (when in Edit Mode)
SHIFT + A - Full Menu (when not in Edit Mode)

SHIFT + Z - wireframe/solid mode

NUM 5 - Ortho Mode

k - knife tool
